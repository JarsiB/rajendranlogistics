Bottom Fly Ash Transport

Bottom ash is the material that collects at the bottom of the furnace, possibly a heavy slag. Because ash characteristics are very different from the front of the boiler to the environmental equipment, the collection, transport and storage systems are usually separate for the furnace and collection points downstream.

Rajendran Logistics operates high-performance new-generation tippers for transportation of bottom fly ash. These trucks are equipped with hydraulic tipping systems and automatic door opening mechanisms for quick, safe and efficient unloading.
