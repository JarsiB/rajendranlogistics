Cement Logistics

Transport of Cement from Cement Factories to Ready Mix Concrete Plants

Ready-mix concrete, or RMC as it's also known, refers to concrete that is specifically batched or manufactured for customers' construction projects, and supplied to the customer on site as a single product. It is a mixture of cements, water and aggregates such as sand, gravel or crushed stone. All aggregates should be of a washed type material with limited amounts of fines or dirt and clay.

An admixture is also added to improve work-ability of the concrete and/or increase setting time of concrete (using retarders) to factor in the time required for the transit mixer to reach the site or place of casting. Ready-mixed concrete is used in construction projects where the construction site is not willing or not able to mix concrete on site. Using ready-mixed concrete ensures the product is delivered finished, on demand, in the specific quantity and mix design required.

We serve as a connecting point for delivering cement from cement factories to Ready Mix Concrete plants in Bengaluru.

We are supplying vehicles to Dalmia Cement authorized transporters for transportation of cement from Dalmia Cement, Ariyalur Plant to IJM Plant, Whitefield / Yelahanka and Shapoorji Plant, Nelamangala, Bengaluru.

Our practice is to ensure that Fly Ash trucks are not deployed for cement transportation and vice versa. Vehicles assigned for a particular material movement are exclusively used for that purpose. This ensures high standards of safety, material integrity and customer satisfaction.
