About Us

Delivering excellence in logistics
since 1974

The company was established in the year 1974 originally in the name S.M. Transports by industry veteran Mr. N. Rajendran, having experience in the field since 1968. The company was majorly focused on transportation of heavy consignments across South India and pioneered movement of heavy equipment to Neyveli Lignite Corporation, Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited and Hindustan Aeronautics Limited. We also possess major experience in transportation of Iron and Steel from SAIL / TATA yards to Tube Products of India (Murugappa Group) and other clients across Tamil Nadu.

In the year of 2010, we entered into Fly Ash & Cement Bulk Transportation. We own a considerable number of trailers with specially designed delivery systems suitable for transportation of bulk cement and bulk dry fly ash. We transport Dry / Wet Fly Ash from Neyveli Thermal Power Station, Neyveli to Ariyalur (Tamil Nadu) cement plants, fly ash brick plants and other industries. We also transport bulk cement from Ariyalur (Tamil Nadu) and ACC Cement (Thondabavei) plants to ready-mix concrete client locations across Karnataka, Tamil Nadu and neighboring states.

Experience is a very valuable asset in the field of transport and logistics, as it provides foresight and helps accurately assess the needs and expenses of each assignment, ensuring smooth and predictable operations. This depth of experience is the pride of Rajendran Logistics, supported by a highly skilled workforce with over 20 years of industry expertise.

Our capabilities truly stand out in extremely rough and hostile environments. Rajendran Logistics is widely known for its expertise in handling bulk cement and fly ash transportation in remote and challenging locations.

Safety remains our highest priority while serving our customers. Our customer-centric approach, professional conduct, and ethical business practices form the foundation of our Code of Conduct.
